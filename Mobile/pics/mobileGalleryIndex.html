About


These are just some drawings that I've made through JavaScript, the code should viewable on my GitHub. These are all just graphs of polar functions, which I think look cool and have good mathematical meaning. The cardioid, for instance, is made simply by having one circle roll around another, and tracing a specific point on the outer circle. They're also formed by time table circles and the mandelbrot set. And I just think that the lemniscate should be saved from the wrists of 22-year-old white women. All of these were a challenge, because I had to use the Cartesian Coordinate system (x and y), instead of a polar coordinate system (radius and angle). So yeah, use them as backgrounds or something.
